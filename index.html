Tripl is a privacy-first expense tracking application designed from the ground up to protect your financial and personal space.

Information We Collect

Tripl does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information on external servers.

All transaction data entered into the application is stored locally on the user's device.

Data Storage

Expense records, budgets, categories, and application preferences are stored exclusively on the user's device.

No cloud synchronization is currently provided.

Analytics and Tracking

Tripl does not use:

Analytics services
Advertising SDKs
User tracking technologies
Third-party data collection services

Data Sharing

Tripl does not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

Permissions

Certain optional features, such as Triple Back Tap gesture detection, may require device permissions related to sensors or foreground services. These permissions are used solely to provide app functionality and are not used to collect personal information.

Data Security

Because user data remains on the device, users maintain direct control over their information. Users are responsible for securing their devices through standard security measures such as screen locks and device encryption.

Children's Privacy

Tripl does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
